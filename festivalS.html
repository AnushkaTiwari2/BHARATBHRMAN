FESTIVALS

THAIPUSAM

Thaipusam

One of the festivals of Tamil Nadu is Thaipusam. It is celebrated on a full moon day in the month of Thai in the Tamil calendar.

PONGAL

Pongal

Pongal is the most famous festival in Tamil Nadu. It is the harvest festival that lasts for four days and is celebrated in the middle of January.

NATYANJALI FESTIVAL

Natyanjali Festival

This festival is celebrated in Nataraja Temple in Chidambaram in Tamil Nadu.

KARTHIGAI DEEPAM

Karthigai Deepam

Karthigai Deepam is one of the festivals of Tamil Nadu, which falls in the month of Karthigai in the Tamil calendar, which is November to mid-December.
